about Pocket Library

A modern book application for the traditional reader.

The classic bibliophile is one who loves to read, admire and collect books, often amassing a large and specialized collection.

Pocket Library was developed to help readers manager their personal libraries. From this app readers can search for books they wish to read and add them to a list they curate for themselves.

Readers can also navigate to a webpage to preview books they are interested in.

Dedicated readers usually have shelves of books in their homes unread. Now readers can add those books to their Pocket Library and track whether it has been read or if it's still on that wishlist.
